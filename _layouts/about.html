About

Located: Chicago, Il

Education: KCAD - BFA Digital Media 2015

About: My fascination with technology goes back as far back as I can remember, some of my earliest memories are of my small fingers hunting and pecking the keys of a MS-DOS computer. From there forward, and somewhere between my first mspaint scribbles and worst photoshop faceswaps, I’ve watched technology grow hopeing that one day I would be able to contribute.

Fast forward a few years, and I am now a designer who is passionate about both software, and design thinking's potential to solve real problems. Throughout my career, school, and personal life, I’ve experimented with a very broad range of technology, but deep down I am still a bit of a Photoshop guy. Recently, I've become quite efficent with Sketch and Axure, and am expanding my horizons by learning basic comp-sci coupled with vanilla Javascript. When I’m not on my computer I usually spend time with my girlfriend, our cat, and dog. I am a lifelong snowboarder, traveler, and artist.
