About

My fascination with technology goes as far back as I can remember, some of my earliest memories are hunting and pecking the keys of a MS-DOS computer. From there forward I’ve watched technology grow hoping that one day I would be able to contribute.

Today, I'm a multidisciplinary designer who is passionate about software. I often find myself pondering about the potential design has to solve problems within and outside of technology. Throughout my career and personal life, I’ve experimented with as many programs as I can. This has allowed me to choose proper tools wisely, to stay efficient, and to feed my curiosity. Deep down I am still a bit of a Photoshop guy. When I’m not on my computer, I usually spend time with my wife, my son, and our two dogs. I am a lifelong snowboarder, traveler, and artist.

If you would like to know anything else, feel free to email me.
